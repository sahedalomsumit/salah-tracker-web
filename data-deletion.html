Data Deletion

Last updated April 06, 2026

This page outlines the data deletion policy and process for the Salah Tracker App. We respect your privacy and provide a straightforward way for you to request the complete removal of your account and associated data.

Warning: Action_is_irreversible

01_ Deletion Process

To request the deletion of your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

01

Send Email

Contact us at sahedalomsumit@gmail.com.

02

Subject Line

Use Data_Deletion_Request as your subject.

03

Account Details

Include the Google Email Address used to sign into the Salah Tracker App.

02_ Data Purge

Authentication

Your Google Sign-In associations, user ID (UID), and synced email address are permanently removed.

Prayer Logs

All synchronized daily prayer entries and timestamps are permanently erased from our databases.
