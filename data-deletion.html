Data Deletion

Last updated April 06, 2026

This page outlines the data deletion policy and process for the Salah Tracker App. We respect your privacy and provide a straightforward way for you to request the complete removal of your account and associated data.

01. HOW TO REQUEST DELETION

To request the deletion of your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

1

Send an Email

Send an email to: sahedalomsumit@gmail.com.

2

Use the Correct Subject Line

Use Data Deletion Request - Salah Tracker as your email subject.

3

Include Your Account Details

Include the Google Email Address you used to sign into the Salah Tracker App.

02. TYPES OF DATA DELETED

  • 👤
    Cloud Authentication Data Your Google Sign-In associations, user ID (UID), and synced email address are permanently removed.
  • 📅
    Prayer Logs & History All synchronized daily prayer entries and timestamps are permanently erased.
